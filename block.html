chemical formula for water

H2O is the chemical formula for water meaning tht each water molecule contains two hydrogen atoms ("H2")and one oxygen atoms("O")

explanation:
H:Represents the element hydrogen.
2: The subscript "2" indicates that there are two hydrogen atoms in a singal water molecule.
O:Represents the elementoxygen
key point about H2O
Covalent bonds   : The hydrogen atoms are connected to the oxygen atoms by covalent bonds where electrons are shared between the atoms.

Bent shape  :Deu to the arrangment of electrons a water molecule has a bent shape

Polar molecule  :Because of its structure water is considered a polar molecule meaning it has a positive end and a negative end.
